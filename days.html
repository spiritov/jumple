jumple is a small passion project that wouldn't exist without initial inspiration from Surfle.

Each jumple 'day' features a randomly selected map from Tempus, a collection of cross-server rocket jumping & sticky jumping maps featuring timer leaderboards and player rankings.

An additional screenshot is given for each map guess or skip, along with a text hint for some. Previous days can be viewed by clicking the calendar icon in the top left. I hope you find jumple fun! Please reach out if you encounter any issues, or would like to help contribute screenshots.

New maps are updated every day at 12:00AM ET (UTC -5).
